## GENERAL FAQ (Optional - can be added as tooltip/modal)

**Q: Can I use the content before getting licensed?**  
A: No. You must wait for license approval before any use.

**Q: What if my application is rejected?**  
A: You'll receive feedback on why and can reapply with corrections.

**Q: Can I negotiate the fees?**  
A: Fees are set by Admin based on standard guidelines, but special cases can be discussed.

**Q: How long does approval take?**  
A: Typically 2-5 business days for initial review.

**Q: Can I transfer my license to another company?**  
A: No, licenses are non-transferable without written approval.

- Use simple, conversational language
- Avoid legal jargon (e.g., say "you can" not "licensee is permitted to")
- Always emphasize community benefit
- Be clear about restrictions upfront
- Use bullet points for easy scanning
- Keep sentences short (under 20 words)
